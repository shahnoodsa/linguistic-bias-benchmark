**Demographics:**

* Age: Late 20s to early 40s
* Gender: Male or female (although men are more likely to be accused of crimes)

* Impulsive: prone to making rash decisions without fully considering the consequences
* Lack of empathy: may not fully understand the impact their actions have on others
